Dear Ciya,

I write this letter along with crafting this site for you to have something to look back to whenever you wish. I truly do hope you like the gift and use it as much as you want.

4 years of knowing you, 1/2 years of being with you and 948 days, 22,752 hours, 1,365,120 minutes, and 81,907,200+ seconds of loving you. An achievement I'm proud having, showing something special and beautiful at the same time between us. May our days have passed by a gentle breeze or a crushing storm, ending it with you was and is a delightful message to the world. Silently saying we can even when we couldn't with one another.

In every memory I have lived to remember, you existing in it made me felt more human than I ever was. Meeting you showed me how it feels to be alive. And for the first time? It wasn't out of pity, but with peace instead. Suddenly, I realized what kind of future I wanted. I want a better life than our families. I want a job that's enjoyable, in payment too! I want a view of the city where the rain sounds nice. I want a room for hobbies, and still be as flexible in creativity as ever. I want pets, 2 or 3 who knows, but what I do know is I want all of it with you. I want to give you that thing you mentioned whenever we just talk. I want to hold you while the rain pours and the music plays in the back. I want to do our hobbies together while we laugh because maybe you really were better than me in a game again. I want so much and so much of you my sweet and I don't regret it.

To say I want us to keep living is both out of love and selfishness, who creeps in pleading you to stay in a universe where we can start the life we've wanted and away from the place who couldn't recognize the bits of your soul and its beauty. Because I wish to praise it. I wish to help you piece the missing pieces you don't have and deserve, along with getting to know the person I'll spend my time with in every day we wake up in. I want finish this hell of a puzzle with you, as I know I have done in our previous lives because in every lifetime we meet, I choose you — always and forever.

You can say, do, push me away countless of times but it will never and I mean never beat the amount of admiration, respect, and of course love I have for you. If you show me the part of you that will make a mess intentionally or not, best believe I am not there to shut you out, but to help you clean it up and hold you tight. And if I get tired? Then I'll rest on you. Because when I mean to say I want you, I want all of you. The you that shows that pretty smile when you smile and hits me in a trance with those eyes. The you that cries when it gets unbearable and your reposts hits a wave of it. The you that gets passionates with her words in any topic, even the smallest ones. The you that knows how to piss someone of, elegantly too like the professional ragebaiter that you are. The you who gets angry too and knows how to stand her business rightfully with the will to understand and learn, slowly and surely. The you who knows how to love me, unknowingly. The you that can change overtime but I'll still fall in love with all over again.

Therefore I think it's safe to say, yes my sweet it's easy to love you, because I love you. And I'll be the one who does. Every. Single Time.

Yours truly,

Li.
